How To Play

Who's Stache is a historical facial hair guessing game.
When starting the game, the player must choose a subset of characters. The characters can be chosen freely, but there shall be a minimum of 10 for the game to start.
"Historical" is a good subset for beginners. Be sure to try "Fictional" as well.
During the game, the player is presented with a beard, and three options for whom it belongs to.
After a guess the character is revealed, and a further question about him (or her!) is asked. A round contains 10 characters for a total of 20 questions.
Every correct answer awards the player 10 points and wrong answer subtracts 5. Your final score can therefore range from -50 to 200.
Good Luck, and thanks for playing our game!
